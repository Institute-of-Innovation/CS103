Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add milk and mix well.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let it cool and enjoy!
